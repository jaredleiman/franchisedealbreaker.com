Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026  ·  Last Updated: January 1, 2026

FranchiseDealBreaker ("we," "us," or "our") operates franchisedealbreaker.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name and email address submitted via our intake forms
  • Franchise Disclosure Documents (FDDs) uploaded for analysis
  • Optional notes or questions you include with your submission
  • Payment information processed through Stripe (we do not store card details)

We also collect standard website analytics data including pages visited, browser type, and referring URLs through anonymized analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to:

  • Process and deliver your requested FDD analysis report
  • Communicate with you about your order status
  • Respond to support inquiries
  • Improve our analysis engine and service quality

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information or your FDD documents with any third parties for marketing purposes.

3. FDD Document Handling

Your uploaded Franchise Disclosure Document is used solely to generate your requested analysis report. Documents are processed by AI systems and are not retained beyond the analysis window. We do not publish, share, or use your submitted FDD for any purpose other than generating your report. All documents are deleted from our systems after your report is delivered.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our business:

  • Stripe — payment processing. Subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Tally — form submission and file upload handling.
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI-powered document analysis.
  • Google Gmail / SendGrid — report delivery via email.

Each third-party service is subject to its own privacy policy. We select services that maintain appropriate data security standards.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. File uploads are handled over encrypted HTTPS connections. We do not store payment card information. However, no internet transmission is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your name and email address for up to 12 months following your purchase for customer support purposes. Uploaded FDD documents are deleted after report delivery. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us at hello@franchisedealbreaker.com.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@franchisedealbreaker.com. We will respond to all requests within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will post the updated policy on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
